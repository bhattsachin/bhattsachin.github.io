Today at 2 PM I found I have accomplished all my goals I had set in the morning. It was an effective day for me with 3 hours to spare. So I am writing this blog sharing an information how you too can achieve it using a todo list.

1.  Prepare a TO DO list

Every day in the morning set up a task list that you are planning to do.

2.  Do not overload yourself

Do not plan everything on your first day. start with small steps & keep on increasing list of tasks as you feel more efficient

3.  Something unavoidable

At times issues come up which might take extra time. So it is always better to break up big task into small tasks and estimate time.

4.  I didn't plan this

It often happens you get a query to solve which you didn't plan early morning, or your boss presents you new task on priority. No worries just add it as part of your day's task & try finishing that first.

5.  what if you missed something

There is always a new day. Just add tasks you missed yesterday on today's task list & depending on that limit your current days list size

6.  Am I doing it right

Your every action should be towards finishing that task list. Keep on asking yourself regularly "what task am I targeting right now".

7.  Just tick it

Keep on clicking your task list as soon you finish it & keep your eyes fixed on unchecked list.

9.  Give your best

Working on a task doesn’t mean you just finish it for the sake of it. Give the best you can offer but do not over do it.

10.  Feel a sense of accomplishment

At the end of the day say to yourself "I have done a great work today". Give a psychological pat to yourself. This will give you energy to keep going.

These steps will help you save time for yourself everyday which you can utilize for your personal development & hobbies.
