Last week I happened to view tag cloud of my favorite web urls. I have been active on delicious for last 2.5 years now. When I looked at the map hence generated, it came as a surprise to me.

This cloud also talks a lot about one's personality & thoughts. One saves bookmarks with a thought that they will be useful in the future. So tag cloud shows how one wants his future to be. I feel it is just a projection of one's mind on a digital format.

Have you seen your tag cloud? Give a try today.
